Chili

Description

Chili is an easy to make dish with beans, ground beef and tomato sauce. You may know how to make it already, but do you know the best options for other ingredients? Read on to find out!

Ingredients

  • 1 can chili beans
  • 1 1lb ground beef
  • 1 onion
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 can tomato sauce
  • Garlic powder
  • Sour cream

Steps

  1. Cook the ground beef.
  2. Dice onions.
  3. Put the ground beef in a pot and add onions and beans. Set to medium heat.
  4. Add diced tomatoes and tomato sauce. Add garlic powder to taste.
  5. When done cooking, serve with a side of sour cream.
