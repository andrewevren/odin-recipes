Oatmeal

Description

Making oatmeal is a very easy way to get your day started. Especially if you go to the store and buy a mix for it! Then all you have to do is put fruits and chocolate on top to have a delicious low effort breakfast.

Ingredients

  • Quaker oats
  • Chocolate chips
  • Raspberries
  • Blueberries

Steps

  1. Pour a packet of Quaker oats into a bowl.
  2. Boil water.
  3. Pour the boiled water onto the oats evenly. Wait 2 minutes.
  4. Add chocolate chips, raspberries and blueberries to your heart's content.
